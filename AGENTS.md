This directory is an Obsidian vault for MLLM study, paper reading, and research notes.

## Purpose

- Maintain a clean, usable knowledge base under `/Users/lingao/mllm`
- Prefer simple Markdown-first changes
- Keep the vault easy to browse and maintain inside Obsidian
- Support research workflows around MLLM and world models

## 用户身份

- 用户是中国科学技术大学的一名研究生
- 主要研究方向包括 MLLM、多模态学习和世界模型
- 笔记内容应主要服务于论文阅读、灵感记录和研究整理

## User Profile

- The user is a graduate student at the University of Science and Technology of China
- Main research areas include MLLM, multimodal learning, and world models
- Notes should generally support paper reading, idea capture, and research organization

## 仓库结构

- `user/`：个人信息、身份说明及与用户本人相关的笔记
- `raw/`：原始论文、来源材料笔记或尚未整理的阅读输入
- `insights/`：研究灵感、总结、提炼后的理解与思考
- `未命名/`：暂未归类的临时笔记，后续可再整理

## Vault Structure

- `user/`: personal notes, profile information, and identity-related content
- `raw/`: original papers, source material notes, or unprocessed reading inputs
- `insights/`: research ideas, takeaways, and refined thinking
- `未命名/`: uncategorized or temporary notes that may be organized later

## 工作规则

- 每次开始工作前，先查看 `AGENTS.md`
- 优先创建或编辑 `.md` 文件，而不是修改 Obsidian 应用配置
- 保留现有中文文件名和内容风格
- 笔记命名保持简短、清晰、可读
- 适合时优先使用 Obsidian 内链，例如 `[[Note Name]]`
- 未明确要求时，避免进行大规模结构调整
- 未明确要求时，不删除用户笔记
- 与个人身份相关的笔记优先放在 `user/`
- 与论文或原始材料相关的笔记优先放在 `raw/`
- 与灵感、总结、综合思考相关的笔记优先放在 `insights/`

## Working Rules

- Before starting any work, check `AGENTS.md` first
- Prefer creating or editing `.md` files rather than changing Obsidian app config
- Preserve existing Chinese filenames and content style
- Keep note names short, clear, and readable
- Use internal Obsidian links like `[[Note Name]]` whenever useful
- Avoid large structural changes unless explicitly requested
- Do not delete user notes unless explicitly asked
- Prefer placing personal profile notes under `user/`
- Prefer placing paper-related or source-material notes under `raw/`
- Prefer placing ideas, summaries, and synthesis notes under `insights/`

## 笔记风格

- 使用简洁的小标题
- 多用项目符号，保证内容便于扫描
- 创建较长的知识笔记时，在开头加入简短摘要
- 对于学习笔记，优先使用以下结构：
- Topic / 主题
- Key ideas / 核心观点
- Examples / 示例
- Questions / 问题
- Related notes / 相关笔记

## Preferred Note Style

- Use concise headings
- Use bullet lists for scannable notes
- Add a short summary at the top when creating longer knowledge notes
- For study notes, prefer sections such as:
- Topic
- Key ideas
- Examples
- Questions
- Related notes

## 在本仓库中协助时

- 如果被要求“初始化”或“搭建”仓库，优先创建实用的起始笔记
- 如果被要求整理内容，优先通过链接建立关系，而不是一次性移动大量文件
- 如果被要求改进工作流，优先建议 Obsidian 原生做法，而不是依赖大量插件
- 创建研究笔记时，优先采用简单、可长期扩展的结构

## When Assisting In This Vault

- If asked to "initialize" or "set up" the vault, create practical starter notes first
- If asked to organize content, prefer linking notes over moving many files at once
- If asked to improve workflow, suggest Obsidian-native patterns before plugin-heavy setups
- When creating research notes, bias toward simple structures that are easy to extend over time
